Privacy Policy

Prithis App — Last updated: April 29, 2026

TL;DR: We collect only the data necessary to provide our location sharing and messaging services. All data transmitted from your device is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell your data to third parties.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Prithis App ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services. By using Prithis App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account info: Display name, email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Profile photo: Avatar image you upload
  • Chat messages: Text, images, and videos you send
  • Friends list: Track IDs of users you connect with

3.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Location data: Real-time GPS coordinates, speed, heading, altitude, and accuracy — only when location sharing is active
  • Location history: Path history retained for up to 24 hours, then automatically purged
  • Device info: GPS provider type (e.g., "gps") for accuracy filtering

3.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: contacts list, call logs, SMS messages, browsing history, device identifiers (IMEI/Android ID), installed apps, or any data beyond what is listed above.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Location sharing: To display your position on a map to friends you've explicitly connected with
  • Messaging: To deliver chat messages, images, and videos between you and your contacts
  • SOS alerts: To broadcast your location to friends in an emergency (expires after 4 hours)
  • Map matching: To snap GPS points to roads for improved accuracy (via Mapbox API)
  • Authentication: To verify your identity via Google Sign-In and JWT tokens
  • Service improvement: Kalman filtering to improve location accuracy

5. Data Encryption & Security

Every piece of data leaving your device is encrypted.
  • In transit: All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption. API requests are authenticated with JWT Bearer tokens.
  • At rest: Location coordinates are stored with AES-256 encrypted representations (encryptedCoords field). MongoDB Atlas encrypts stored data and backups.
  • Authentication: Passwords are hashed (bcrypt). Google Sign-In uses OAuth 2.0. JWT tokens expire after 7 days.
  • Image storage: Photos uploaded to Cloudinary are served over HTTPS with auto-compression and format optimization.
  • Access control: Users can only access their own data. Friend location visibility requires mutual connection.

7. Data Retention

  • Location path history: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • SOS alerts: Automatically expire after 4 hours
  • Chat messages: Retained until you or the recipient delete them
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted upon account deletion request.
  • Images: Stored on Cloudinary until message deletion or account removal

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Location control: You can stop sharing your location at any time by disabling location sharing in the app. Background location requires explicit foreground service notification.
  • Privacy modes: Choose who can see your location — Everyone, Contacts Only, or Selected friends only.
  • Block users: Block any user from seeing your location or contacting you.
  • Data deletion: Request full account and data deletion by contacting us.
  • Permission revocation: You can revoke any permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Prithis → Permissions.

9. Children's Privacy

Prithis App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Users

Your data may be processed and stored on servers outside your country of residence. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to our servers and third-party services (MongoDB Atlas, Cloudinary, Mapbox) which may operate in different jurisdictions.

11. Changes to This Policy
